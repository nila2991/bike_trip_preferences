# Cyclistic Membership Analysis

## Business Question
How do annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bikes differently?

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Cyclistic bike-share trip data to identify behavioral differences between annual members and casual riders and develop recomendations for increasing memberships. 

## Dataset
Historical Cyclistic trip data (~5 million trips over one year).

## Tools
- R
- tidyverse
- ggplot2
- lubridate

## Key Findings
- Casual riders use Cyclistic bikes most frequently on weekends and during the summer months.
- Casual riders have longer average ride durations than annual members.
- Annual members show a more consistent riding pattern throughout both the week and the year.

## Interpretation
- Casual riders appear to use the service primarily for leisure and recreational activities.
- Annual members appear to rely on the service more regularly, suggesting transportation and commuting purposes.

## Recommendations
- Launch targeted membership campaigns during weekends and summer months, when casual rider activity is highest.
- Explore and test alternative membership options, such as weekend-only or seasonal memberships, to better align with the usage patterns of casual riders.
